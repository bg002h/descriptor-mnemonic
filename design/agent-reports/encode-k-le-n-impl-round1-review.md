Comparison is `>` (not `>=`); fields match `error.rs:108` `KGreaterThanN { k: u8, n: usize }`; the comparison shape `*k as usize > len` mirrors decode's `k as usize > count` at `tree.rs:247-248` (Multi-family) and `:259-260` (Thresh).
