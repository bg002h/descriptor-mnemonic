## CRITICAL

### C-1: tap compile produced invalid `tr(pk(@0))` (FIXED post-review)

`compile_policy_to_template` for `ScriptContext::Tap` blindly wrapped the compiled miniscript with
`tr({ms})`. For `pk(@0)`, miniscript Tap renders as `pk(@0)`, producing `tr(pk(@0))` which is
invalid (tr's first arg must be a key, not a `pk()` expression). Confirmed by reproducing:
`md encode --from-policy pk(@0) --context tap` → `internal key must have no children, but found 1`.

**Fix applied:** detect single-key `pk(...)` form and emit `tr({inner})` directly (collapses to
`tr(@0)` which round-trips through `parse_template`). Multi-leaf policies return a clear
CompileError noting the v0.15 limitation (would require NUMS internal key, out of scope).

**New test:** `compile_pk_tap_emits_keypath_only` covers the fix.

### C-2: `--context tap` had no integration test (FIXED post-review)

Added `compile_pk_tap_emits_keypath_only`.
